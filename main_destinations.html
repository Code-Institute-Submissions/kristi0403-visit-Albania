Saranda

Sarandë is a coastal town in Albania.Saranda typically has over 300 sunny days a year.The city is known for its blue deep waters . Near Sarandë are the remains of the ancient city of Butrint, a UNESCO World Heritage site.

Pogradeci

Pogradec is a city and municipality in central Albania, situated on the shores of the Ohrid lake.This city is surrounded by hills on the southern and western side. The lake is in the eastern and northern side of the city.

Durres

Durrës is a port city on the Adriatic Sea in western Albania, west of the capital, Tirana. It’s known for its huge Roman amphitheater. Nearby is a 9th-century church with mosaic-covered walls.

Fieri

Fier is a city and a municipality in Fier County in southwest Albania. Fier is 11 km from the ruins of the ancient city of Apollonia. The city was founded in 588 BCE .

Shkoder

Shkodër is one of the most ancient cities in the Balkans and exerts strong influences in culture, religion, arts and entertainment of northern Albania.

Berat

Berat is a city on the Osum River, in central Albania. It's known for its white Ottoman houses. On a hilltop, Berat Castle is a huge compound now inhabited by townspeople.

Elbasan

Elbasan is the third largest city in Albania.The Albanian name is derived from the Ottoman Turkish il-basan ("the fortress").

Korca

Korça, in the southeastern region of Albania, is one of the most beautiful and important cities in the country. Because of its beauty, French-style villas and coffee culture, Korça is also called the 'little Paris of Albania'.

Kruja

Krujë is a town located in the North central of Albania.Krujë was inhabited by the ancient Illyrian tribe of the Albani. In 1190 Krujë became the capital of the first Albanian state in the middle ages, the Principality of Arbër.

Lezhë is a town in northwest Albania. The city was one of the main centres of the Illyrian kingdom. The tomb of Gjergj Kastriot Skenderbej is located there.On 2 march 1944 with Skanderbeg as leader the Albanian chiftains united against the Ottoman Empire. This was called the League of Lezhë.

Tirana

Tirana is the capital and largest city by area and population of the Republic of Albania. It is located in the center of Albania enclosed by mountains and hills with Mount Dajt elevating on the east and a slight valley on the northwest overlooking the Adriatic Sea in the distance.

Tropoja
