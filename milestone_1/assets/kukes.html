Kukes

The terrain of the county consists of small plains and high mountains. The county lies almost entirely within the basin of the Drin.Lake Fierza and Koman lies in the county and are fed and drained by rivers Black Drin and White Drin.

The waterfall of Valbona

Valbona Waterfall, located not far from Gjelaj Village, is another must-see whilst in the area! The Bajram Curri road towards the village of Shoshan, will lead you to Shoshan Canyon which hides another beautiful waterfall within.
