Albania

Capital and largest city is Tirana. Official language is Albanian. Currency is Lek (ALL). Time zone UTC+1 (CET).Driving side is right. Albania rises into mountains and hills that run in different directions across the length and breadth of its territory. Among the most remarkable features about the country is the presence of numerous important lakes.Its shores faces the ADriatic sea and the Ionian sea.The weather is mostly mediteranean and continental with 4 distinct seasons.
