Benja a village in elbasan with thermal waters.

Rich in minerals and healing properties, the thermal baths of Albania in nowadays Elbasan, or Benja were mentioned since the Roman period. Thermal baths and springs in Albania can be found in different parts of the country.

Sotira's waterfall in Gramsh / Elbasan

The top attractions to visit in Gramsh are: Sotira Waterfall , Holta Canyon and Lake of Dushkut.The top activities are :Tomorr National Park / National Park & Sotira waterfall Hiking /Adventure Spirit - 4X4.

The village of Gjinar

Gjinar, a mountainous village 23 kilometers from Elbasan, is frequented every season by tourists.Hotels are already offering a great experience. Family tourism is expected to get a boost after the “100 Villages” project, which will assist the infrastructure of 100 Albanian villages in order to improve tourism.

Ethnographic museum

Located in the center of the city, not far from the Castle of Elbasan, this museum is housed in a traditional two-story 18th century house that tells of the culture, folklore and lifestyle in this medieval city.
